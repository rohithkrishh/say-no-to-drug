About Our Campaign

Our campaign aims to create awareness about the dangers of drug abuse and promote a healthier, safer Kerala. We believe in the power of education, prevention, and community support.

Awareness

We provide accurate information about drugs and their harmful effects on individuals, families, and communities.

Education

We conduct educational programs in schools, colleges, and communities to teach young people about the risks of drug use.

Support

We provide resources and support for those affected by drug addiction, helping them find the path to recovery.
